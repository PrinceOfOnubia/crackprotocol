Privacy Policy

Crack Protocol collects limited information required to operate adversarial challenge environments and leaderboard systems.

We intentionally minimize data collection wherever possible.

Information Collected

The platform may collect:

  • usernames
  • wallet addresses
  • challenge interaction logs
  • leaderboard activity
  • browser/session storage data
  • basic analytics and security logs

Wallet Addresses

Wallet addresses are currently used for identity continuity and future reward compatibility.

Wallet addresses may appear publicly on leaderboards or challenge records in shortened form.

Local Storage

Current identity persistence may use browser local storage to maintain session continuity between visits.

Users may clear locally stored data at any time through browser settings or protocol reset controls.

Challenge Logs

Challenge interactions may be recorded for:

  • exploit verification
  • security analysis
  • leaderboard scoring
  • protocol research
  • defensive system improvement

Data Usage

Collected information is used solely to:

  • operate the protocol
  • manage challenge participation
  • improve defensive systems
  • analyze adversarial behavior
  • distribute rewards where applicable

We do not sell personal data.

Security

Crack Protocol uses reasonable operational safeguards to protect platform integrity and stored information.

However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

Future Updates

As protocol infrastructure evolves, this policy may be updated to reflect:

  • wallet integrations
  • onchain systems
  • agent deployment infrastructure
  • replay systems
  • ranking systems
